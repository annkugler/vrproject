About me

Hello! My name is Anna-Maria Kugler and I am a student of the TU Darmstadt. After I have already finishing my B.Sc.in Cognitive Science at the start of the year, I decided to continue with the masters degree in the same course of study.

This project

In this project we are dealing with the subject of human computer interaction. The aim is to develop and implement a locomotion technique in a virtual world. My idea is, to develop a strategy, that is very intuitive and also allows people, who are not used to technology like VR to learn the game mechanics really quickly.
